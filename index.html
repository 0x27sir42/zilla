About Zila Token

What is Zila?

Zila is a utility token built on the Polygon network, designed to power a decentralized ecosystem for peer-to-peer transactions, staking, and governance. It enables fast, low-cost transfers while providing holders with voting rights on platform developments.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading decentralized finance token that democratizes access to financial services globally.

Mission: To build a secure, scalable, and community-driven ecosystem that empowers users with full control over their digital assets through innovative DeFi solutions.

Transaction Fees

2% fee on all transactions: 1% burned to reduce supply, 1% distributed to staking pool holders.

Staking

Users can stake ZILA tokens to earn passive rewards (up to 25% APY) and gain voting rights on governance proposals.

Governance

Stakeholders can propose and vote on platform updates, new features, and partnership decisions to shape Zila's future.
